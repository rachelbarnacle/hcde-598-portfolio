I work at Boeing, on the company's HR website as a functional/business analyst: collecting, refining, and translating requirements, testing, and trying to interject with user-friendly suggestions and improvements when I can get a soapbox to stand on (only because I'm short, not because people won't listen). HW 2: Lego Family

HW 3: Monster Cat

A character whose eyes will follow the user's mouse position. If the user clicks on the canvas, the character moves up and down (and the eyes will not follow the user's mouse). If the user clicks the canvas again, the character's eyes will begin to follow the user's mouse position.

HW 4b: Random tint to picture

HW5: Data Viz - cereal

A data visualization of cereals, plotting the cereal's sugar content vs. fiber content. Hovering over points provides more details about each cereal, while points are color-coded based on a score determined by Consumer Reports.
